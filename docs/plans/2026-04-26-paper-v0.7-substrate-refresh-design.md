> *"A provenance record π accompanies each datom; v0.4.0a1 ships its typed schema as the `Provenance` `TypedDict` (`total=False`) over seven known keys — `source`, `tx_time`, `handler_id`, `canonical_call`, `parent_provenance_hash`, `superseded_by_tx`, and a free-form `extra` map for caller-defined fields.
